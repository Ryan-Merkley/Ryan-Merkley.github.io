About

My name is Ryan Merkley and I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, enrolled in the Global Business and Digital Arts program.

Being apart of GBDA has given me the opportunity to acquire 4 years of design experience and managerial techniques. This includes areas such as storyboarding, prototyping, team-building, and task management. I have also gained 4 years of Graphic Design experience using Adobe Creative Suite software. These experiences have piqued my interest for a career in design and/or project management.

As a dedicated and motivated professional, I consistently make strides to enhance the quality of my work by broadening my skillset and discovering new areas to grow. Every project in this portfolio whether it be independent, or collaborative has allowed me to grow and understand how to be successful in the real world.

You can check out some of my previous projects that I have created on this site.
