ABOUT

Hello and welcome to my ePortfolio page. My name is Ryan Merkley and I'm currently an undergraduate student at the University of Waterloo, enrolled in the Global Business and Digital Arts program.

Over the past couple of years, I've had experience with coding, and visual design through programs such as Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop for school projects as well as for a 3 story micro tiles wall.

During my free time, I enjoy playing video games as well I like to volunteer for different community events around my local area. Volunteerism is an important aspect in my life, as it gives me the opportunity to give back to my community as well as participate in events that help change the quality of life for many individuals.

I am a very dedicated and responsible person when it comes to my work. I enjoy challenging myself and learning new skills and strategies that can help improve the quality of my work.
