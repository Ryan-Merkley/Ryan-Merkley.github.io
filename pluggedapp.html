APP BRAND DEVELOPMENT: PLUGGED

Upon completion of our prototype website for Plugged, our next challenge was to create a prototype mobile application. The purpose of this project was to understand the basics of an app on a smart device within the context of a brand development strategy. We were responsible for using the information that was given to us from our prototype website to develop a digital application.

In addition to creating our mobile application, we were required to come up with at least one more brand feature that would help distinguish the app from the website. When building this app, our main focus was on making sure that our app felt like its own independent function rather than just being a duplicate of our website. Most of the content from our website is on our app, however with the addition of two new features which included a check-in option and a reward rating system. These new features were created to benefit all three types of users while promoting increased engagement and usage of our app. The check-in feature allows fans and audience users to confirm their presence at an event from their mobile device and grant them access to rate the event based on the entertainment and the venue itself. The submission of this rating is then rewarded with a promotional code granting users perks such as free or discounted food, drinks, tickets, and merchandise to future shows. These ratings give the artists and venues feedback on their respective performance while the check-ins provide valuable statistics on fans using our app to reach their events.

Throughout the designing stage, we used user testing to give us the ability to improve upon certain features of our app. We were able to improve on some technical issues such as increasing the size of the 'back' button, update our rating system, and introduce a comment section for fans on their respective artists.
